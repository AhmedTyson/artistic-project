RECOMMENDED IMAGE SIZE
======================

For the best results on the CERA Products page, use:

**1024 x 1024 pixels (Square)**

Why?
- The image gallery is responsive.
- On Mobile, the container is often taller (portrait).
- On Desktop, the container is often wider (landscape).
- A Square (1:1) aspect ratio ensures the center of the image is always visible and looks great on all screens.
