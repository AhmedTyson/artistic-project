# Image Generation Prompts for New Products

## General Settings
- **Aspect Ratio:** Square (1:1) is recommended for product cards.
- **Style:** High-quality product photography, photorealistic, sharp focus.

---

## 1. Product 7: Grandma's Handcrafted Statue
**Theme:** Rustic, sentimental, imperfect, "hobby art".

- **Slide 1 (Main Product - White Background):**
  > A rustic, handcrafted clay statue of a small abstract figure or animal (like a cat or bird), painted with simple colors, showing imperfections and visible brushstrokes. Reminiscent of a grandmother's hobby art. Soft lighting, isolated on a pure white background, high-resolution product photography.

- **Slide 2 (Detail Texture):**
  > Close-up macro shot of the painted clay texture on the handmade statue. Visible fingerprints in the clay, slightly uneven paint application, warm and nostalgic feel. Sharp focus on the texture.

- **Slide 3 (Lifestyle Context):**
  > The handcrafted clay statue sitting on a vintage wooden side table next to a lace doily and a pair of reading glasses. Cozy, warm ambient lighting, cottagecore aesthetic, blurred background of a living room.

## 2. Product 8: Sofa Graphic T-Shirt
**Theme:** Modern apparel, clean, vintage graphic.

- **Slide 1 (Main Product - White Background):**
  > A high-quality white cotton crewneck t-shirt. On the chest is a detailed, vintage-style illustration of a floral patterned sofa. Flat lay photography, smooth fabric, isolated on a white background.

- **Slide 2 (Detail Texture):**
  > Extreme close-up of the t-shirt fabric showing the cotton weave and the print quality of the sofa illustration. The floral pattern on the sofa graphic is intricate and colorful.

- **Slide 3 (Lifestyle Context):**
  > A person wearing the white t-shirt with the sofa graphic, standing casually in a modern, sunlit room. The focus is on the shirt design. Natural lighting, depth of field with a blurred background.

## 3. Product 9: Pharaoh Antique Artifact
**Theme:** Ancient, golden, museum-quality, historical.

- **Slide 1 (Main Product - White Background):**
  > An ancient Egyptian golden statue artifact (e.g., a pharaoh's head or sacred cat). Weathered gold texture with patina, intricate hieroglyphic engravings. Museum-quality artifact, isolated on a stark white background, professional lighting.

- **Slide 2 (Detail Texture):**
  > Macro photography of the weathered gold surface of the ancient artifact. Highlighting the cracks, patina, and detailed engravings of hieroglyphs. richness and history.

- **Slide 3 (Lifestyle Context):**
  > The golden pharaoh artifact displayed on a black velvet pedestal inside a glass museum case. Dramatic spotlighting from above, dark and elegant atmosphere, reflections on the glass.
